About Me

I'm a full stack web developer with a passion for coding, absorbing new skills, and collaborating with other people. I'm looking for challenging, meaningful, and interesting projects that I can contribute to while simultaneously expanding my skills as a developer.


I previously worked at an e-commerce startup from the ground floor. I started as a Content Writer and transitioned into a management role. I was also responsible for general SEO maintenance including best on-page practices and outreach for off-page SEO.


I taught myself HTML and CSS to publish articles for the company's website. I soon became fascinated with the entire coding process from developing the original idea to the final working product. It was clear to me that my career path should involve coding, and enrolling in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp was the first step to achieve my goal.


I bring to any endeavor complete and focused dedication, the ability to work with others as part of a team, and a flexible mindset for achieving the success of any project.
